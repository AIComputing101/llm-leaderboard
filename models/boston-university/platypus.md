## 🔧 Technical Specifications

### Model Architecture
- **Type:** Dense
- **Parameters:** 70.0B
- **Training Tokens:** 2000.0B
- **Token:Param Ratio:** 29:1 ✅ Compute-optimal

### Training Efficiency
- **ALScore:** 1.2 (Mid-tier)
- **Formula:** √(Parameters × Tokens) ÷ 300
