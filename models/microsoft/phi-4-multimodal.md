## 🔧 Technical Specifications

### Model Architecture
- **Type:** Dense
- **Parameters:** 5.6B
- **Training Tokens:** 6100.0B
- **Token:Param Ratio:** 1,090:1 ✅ Compute-optimal

### Training Efficiency
- **ALScore:** 0.6 (Lightweight)
- **Formula:** √(Parameters × Tokens) ÷ 300

### Training Data
- **Dataset Type:** synthetic, web-scale
